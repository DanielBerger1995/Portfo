About Me
Multimedia Designer & Front-End Developer
Hi there, my name is Daniel Berger.
I am a creative, artistic individual with a passion for solving real world problems with the use of innovative digital solutions. My motivation has always been to create unique and memorable experiences by utilizing user empathy and loveable designs.

I can confidently say that I thrive in a team environment and love bouncing ideas off like minded people, making sure that everyone in the team feels included and has space to share their voice. On top of that, the past three years working in a startup company has taught me to be organized, responsible and independent.
